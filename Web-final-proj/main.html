Tic Tac Toe

a game in which two players alternately put Xs and Os in compartments of a figure
formed by two vertical lines crossing two horizontal lines and each tries to get a row
of three Xs or three Os before the opponent does

Snake Game

The so-called snake is a line that moves like a snake. You control it by pressing the arrows to get the food (the dots).
Whenever the snake eats the food, it grows a little bit. If the snake head touches its body, game over.
